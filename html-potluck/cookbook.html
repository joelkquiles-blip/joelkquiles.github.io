Recipe 1: Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • Angel Hair Pasta
  • Chicken Breast
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta to al dente.
  2. Cook the chicken breast with personal seasoning.
  3. Mix pasta, chicken, and sauce together.
  4. Top with parmesan and serve.

Recipe 2: Tacos

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Salsa

Instructions

  1. Cook the ground beef with seasoning.
  2. Warm the tortillas.
  3. Add ground beef first, then add toppings.
  4. Serve and enjoy.

Recipe 3: Pancakes

Ingredients

  • Pancake mix
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients together.
  2. Pour batter on a hot buttered pan.
  3. Flip when bubbles form on the batter.
  4. Serve with syrup and enjoy.
